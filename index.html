How It Works

From arrival to payment — fully tracked.

GarageOS follows the natural flow of your workday. No complicated setup, no IT skills needed.

01
Customer arrives
Find the customer in seconds or create a new record. Vehicle details, contact info, and full history are right there.
02
Open a service card
Create a service card for the visit. Add individual jobs to it — each assigned to a technician — and track parts and time as work progresses.
03
Complete the service
Log all services provided and parts used. Stock is updated automatically. Job status changes to done.
04
Send invoice & get paid
One click generates a complete invoice from the job data. Customer pays, transaction is recorded. Done.
